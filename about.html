About Us

Why Bagels?

Growing up in New York, The bagel capital of the world, we would pick up delicious bagels to start our long ski trips. The bagel was the perfect breakfast to give us the energy we needed to ski the icy mountains in the East. When we fell in love with Colorado, we wanted to bring our bagel recipes to the Rockies. Whether it's hiking, bike riding, or skiing, bagels are our favorite way to start the day.

Meet Lando!

Lando is the brains of the operation. Not only is he adorable, but he loves hiking in FoCo and inspired us to make a bagel shop.
